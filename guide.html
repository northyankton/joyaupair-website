Pre-Arrival Checklist

Required Documents

  • ✓ Valid passport (at least 6 months validity)
  • ✓ Chinese visa (we will assist with the application)
  • ✓ Medical examination records
  • ✓ Insurance documentation
  • ✓ Original educational certificates

What to Pack

  • ✓ Seasonal clothing
  • ✓ Personal care items
  • ✓ Medications (if needed)
  • ✓ Electronics and adapters
  • ✓ Photos and mementos from home

Preparations

  • ✓ Learn basic Chinese phrases
  • ✓ Research Chinese culture
  • ✓ Contact your host family
  • ✓ Download useful Chinese apps
  • ✓ Prepare teaching materials

Arrival Process

1. Airport Arrival

Our staff will meet you at the airport holding a JoyAupair sign. We'll help with luggage and transportation to your host family.

2. Meeting Your Host Family

You'll be introduced to your host family and shown to your room. Take time to rest and get settled in.

3. Orientation Program

Within the first week, you'll attend our orientation program covering:

  • • Cultural adjustment tips
  • • Local transportation
  • • Emergency procedures
  • • Program guidelines
  • • Chinese language basics
