Au Pair in China

Experience Chinese Culture While Living with a Host Family

What is Au Pair?

An Au Pair is a young person who lives with a host family and helps with childcare and light housework in exchange for room, board, and cultural experience.

In China, Au Pairs have the unique opportunity to experience Chinese culture firsthand while helping families with English language education.
